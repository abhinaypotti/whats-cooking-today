# Masala Chai (tea)

## Photos

## Serves 
1 Cup

## Ingredients
* 1 Cup Milk
* 10% Water in propotion of Milk
* Tea - 1 tea Spoon 
* Sugar - 0 Spoon (I suggest to go with sugerless for best taste and health)
* Ginger - 1 inch

## Recipe
* Take a Vessel for tea making
* add Milk and Water
* Boil it 
* Add crushed Ginger
* Add Tea and Sugar
* Boil till color changes
* Strain through and ready for sip!
